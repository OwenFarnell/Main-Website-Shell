Wifi_Penetration_Hack Tutorial

1. Connect ESP32 Chip to laptop/PC using a micro usb port

2. Make sure red light on ESP32 chip is on


3. If correctly connected, open your wifi list and connect to ManagementAP


4. When it asks you for a password, type mgmtadmin


5. If you do not see ManagementAP on your wifi list, click Hidden Netowork and follow steps 3 and 4 again.


6. Open up your browser of choice (aside from tor)


7. In search bar, type 192.168.4.1 or http://192.168.4.1/


8. Then let it load all networks in range, choose your penetration hack and time, then click attack
