WiFi Penetration Hack Tutorial

Follow the steps below to perform the WiFi penetration hack using ESP32:

  1. Connect the ESP32 chip to your laptop/PC using a micro USB port.
  2. Make sure the red light on the ESP32 chip is turned on.
  3. If correctly connected, open your WiFi list and connect to "ManagementAP".
  4. When prompted for a password, enter "mgmtadmin".
  5. If you do not see "ManagementAP" on your WiFi list, click "Hidden Network" and repeat steps 3 + 4.
  6. Open your preferred browser (except Tor).
  7. In the browser's address bar, type "192.168.4.1" or "http://192.168.4.1/".
  8. Wait for the page to load and display all networks in range.
  9. Choose the desired penetration hack and set the time, then click "Attack".
